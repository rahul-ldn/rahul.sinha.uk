Alpha House Directions

published on Monday, 9 September 2019

From the Tube, or points North and East

  • From Brixton Road, go west along Ferndale
  • Turn left on Allardyce
  • Walk straight to the Gate at the end of the street

From points South

  • Walk up Brixton Road
  • Turn left on to Baytree
  • Cross Acre Lane, and walk up Concannon, which begins to the left
  • Turn right on Santley
  • Turn right into the Gate at Allardyce

From points West

If coming through northern Clapham

  • Come down Ferndale
  • Turn right on Allardyce
  • Walk straight to the Gate at the end of the street

If coming through southern Clapham

  • Come up Acre Lane
  • Turn left on Concannon
  • Turn right on Santley
  • Turn right into the Gate at Allardyce

From the Gate

  • Right the appropriate flat from the Gate call box
  • Once the door is unlocked, walk to the left and around the building.
  • Walk to the entrance labelled "Girls" and ring the appropriate flat from the exterior door call box.
  • The common area light is on your right.
